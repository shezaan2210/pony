Pony was born from a dream, that of telling beautiful stories about beautiful brands.
Our mission is to give even more meaning to what you do and to reveal yourself to the world.
We are aware that it is sometimes difficult to talk about yourself or your project.
But we believe in the power of listening and paying attention to help you move from head talk to heart talk.
By taking the time to discuss, to laugh, sometimes to cry, to find together the most accurate words, the most inspiring image and the most effective actions for your brand or your business.

who is pony?

My name is Rébecca Chemouil, I was born in 1979 and I founded Pony in 2019. When I was little, I dreamed of saving gorillas and being a French teacher.

I took a slight detour, finally spending twenty years in marketing and communications, in agencies and with advertisers.

It was out of a deep desire to move the boundaries and assume that she wasn't like anyone that Pony was born. Far from marketing clichés and dogmas, I adopt a unique, joyful and authentic style in order to support clients from all backgrounds and meet wonderful people so that I never get bored. I like to be alongside those who have the courage to create, to build, to invent, to help them reveal themselves.

I listen, I analyze and I identify the smallest detail, even the most buried, to make each subject a unique case in order to build together a positioning, an identity, a storytelling and a strategy that only resembles you .

Why this model?
To always have the freedom to truly choose who to work with. Each team is made up
of talent and feeling, so that everyone is in their zone of genius and gives the best of themselves.
And if it is necessary to call on another agency, we happily collaborate with the most inspiring and expert people possible.
Beyond skills, the subject must animate everyone, make them vibrate.
The client's state of mind and method must match those of the stakeholders, in order to reveal the best of all, and make each project a great adventure.
